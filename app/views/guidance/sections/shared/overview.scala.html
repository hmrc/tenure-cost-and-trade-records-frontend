The Valuation Office Agency (VOA) is responsible for valuing every non-domestic property in England and Wales. To do this, the VOA sends forms of return to non-domestic premises which must be completed by the business or organisation operating from the property.

People who should complete these declarations include:

  • businesses or organisations who own and occupy the property
  • businesses or organisations who occupy the property under a lease or agreement
  • landlords of a vacant property where no lease or agreement is in place

You can also ask third parties to complete the declaration on your behalf.

Declarations are divided into different sections. The amount of information you need to provide in each section will depend on:

  • whether your business or organisation owns or rents the property
  • the complexity of your business or organisation
