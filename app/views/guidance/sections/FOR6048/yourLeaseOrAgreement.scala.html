@messages("label.section.aboutYourLeaseOrTenure")

If your business owns the property, or occupies the property on a permanent rent-free arrangement, you will not have to fill out this section.

What you'll be asked

We need to know:

  • your landlord’s details
  • details of your lease
  • if any items or services are included in your rent
  • how the rent was calculated
  • if any exclusions, restrictions or incentives are included in your lease or agreement

What you’ll need

You should have:

  • a copy of your lease
  • a record of any rent reviews
  • if applicable, details of any improvements you have made to the property which increased its value, such as parking, extensions, or extensive renovations

Important dates

When:

  • the current rent was first paid under the terms of the lease or agreement
  • the current lease began
  • the current lease was agreed or set
  • the next rent revue is due, if applicable
