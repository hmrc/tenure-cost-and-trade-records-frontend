What you'll be asked

You must tell us:

  • if the property is vacant
  • the name of the business or organisation operating from the property
  • whether the business or organisation owns or rents the property
  • if the declaration is being completed by a third party

What you'll need

If the property is vacant, but you receive rental income from lettings such as phone masts, advertising billboards or car parking, you'll need those tenancy agreements.

Important dates

If the property is vacant, we need to know when it became vacant.
