@messages("label.section.aboutYourTradingHistory")

What you'll be asked

How much:

  • revenue your self-catering holiday accommodation business generates
  • it costs to operate your business

What you'll need

If your business has occupied the property for longer than 3 years
You'll need trading figures from the 3 most recent financial years.

If your business has occupied the property for less than 3 years
You’ll be asked for figures from all completed financial years since you occupied the property.

You’ll only be asked for figures from the categories listed here. If any information we need does not apply to your property, enter the number 0.

  • Accounting information
    Your VAT registration status and your financial year end
  • Income
    Including all letting income and income from providing services such as electricity, laundry or meals
  • Fixed costs
    Details of costs such as insurance, business rates or rent
  • Cost of sales
    Details of any costs incurred
  • Accounting costs
    Details of any wages, National Insurance, depreciation, accountancy fees and bank charges
  • Administrative costs
    Including subscriptions, telephone and broadband, booking agency fees, advertising and printing
  • Operational costs
    Including any utilities, laundry and cleaning, repairs and maintenance, TV licences, travelling expenses and other costs

Important dates

When your financial year ends.
