What you'll be asked

You must tell us:

  • if any parts of the property are used as a permanent residence by tenants or employees
  • if any parts of the property are used for commercial lets of 29 nights or longer
  • how many nights you intend to let the property in the coming year
  • details of your letting season
  • if you advertise online

What you'll need

Records containing any:

  • details of any permanent residents
  • details of any commercial lets of 29 nights or longer
  • URLs of the websites where you advertise, and any property reference number if known

Important dates

If you have stopped letting the property commercially, we need to know the date when you last hosted a paying guest.
